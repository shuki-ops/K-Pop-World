BTS

by Karina Kinashenko

Posted on June 2, 2020 at 17:03 PM

Taehyun (태현) is a South Korean singer-songwriter and rapper under Big Hit Entertainment. He is a member of the boy group TXT.

On January 17, Taehyun was revealed as the fourth member of Big Hit Entertainment's newest boy group TXT.[1] He made his official debut with the group on March 4, 2019 with their debut mini album, The Dream Chapter: Star.

TXT and fans bring together every piece of their dreams to complete their own, one dream.
